Introduction

WanderWise is an intelligent travel recommendation web application designed to help users discover their next adventure. By analyzing user preferences such as climate, activities, and budget, WanderWise suggests ideal countries and destinations tailored to individual interests. With a sleek interface, real-time travel data, and personalized recommendations, WanderWise simplifies the travel planning process, allowing users to explore new destinations effortlessly and wisely.
